Unlocking Hope: The Superior Efficacy of rTMS for Treatment-Resistant Depression

In a groundbreaking Harvard-led study, researchers such as Dr. Alexander Bystritsky have discovered a highly effective treatment for individuals suffering from Treatment-Resistant Depression (TRD). The study compared three different treatment plans and found that repetitive Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (rTMS) combined with existing antidepressant medications significantly reduced depressive symptoms more than other treatment options. This new approach offers hope to those who have exhausted traditional therapies, highlighting rTMS as a promising solution for TRD.
