Our approach

At Westside NeuroTherapeutics, we don't just treat symptoms, we treat individuals. Our approach to Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) therapy goes beyond the conventional. We understand that each brain is as unique as the person it belongs to, which is why we personalize every aspect of your treatment. Personalization at Its Core: From your first consultation, we're committed to understanding your unique journey with depression. Our psychiatrists perform a thorough evaluation to craft a treatment plan that is as individual as you are.

Precision Targeted Treatment: Utilizing cutting-edge technology, we ensure the magnetic pulses of TMS therapy are precisely delivered. This precision means we can directly target the areas of your brain that need rejuvenation, offering you the best possible outcomes.
Committed to Comfort: We believe in making your path to recovery as comfortable and stress-free as possible. Our treatment sessions are designed to be a peaceful time for healing and with no downtime, you can continue with your day right after.
Transparent and Supportive: From navigating insurance coverage to understanding the treatment process, we're with you every step of the way. We ensure our therapy is accessible, understandable, and supported by the latest research.
Your Partner in Healing: Our goal is not only to provide you with the most effective treatment but also to empower you to reclaim your life from depression. With Westside NeuroTherapeutics, you're not just receiving treatment, you're gaining a partner dedicated to your well-being.
