Our Approach

At Westside NeuroTherapeutics, we don't just treat symptoms, we treat individuals. Our approach to Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation therapy goes beyond the conventional. We understand that each brain is as unique as the person it belongs to, which is why we personalize every aspect of your treatment.

Personalized Care:
Your journey starts with a consultation designed to understand your needs and your story. From there, our team of psychiatrists create a treatment plan tailored specifically to you.

Targeted Treatment:
Our FDA-approved TMS technology delivers magnetic pulses directly to the areas of your brain that need rejuvenation. This precision ensures we can offer you the best possible outcomes.

Committed to Comfort:
We believe recovery should feel safe, comfortable, and stress-free. That’s why our sessions are calm, non-invasive, and medication-free. Our treatment plan is designed to be a peaceful time for healing and with no downtime, you can continue with your day right after! From navigating insurance, keeping updated with the latest research, along with walking you through every detail – our team is here to support you through each step.

Your Partner in Healing:
When you choose Westside NeuroTherapeutics, you’re not just starting a treatment — you’re gaining a partner committed to helping you reclaim your life and rediscover what well-being feels like.
